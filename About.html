Sawyer Dickinson started painting as a hobby in 2021. Since then, he has created an array of pieces that vary in medium, subject matter, and style. He's always experimenting and trying to find the right style for his work. Sawyer ocassionally showcases his work at art exhibits and shows when he is able to. Sawyer's biggest influences in the art world are Van Gogh, Jasper Cropsey, Freedrick Church, Paul Gauguin, Peter Bruegel the Elder, Rembrandt, Renior, Paul Cezanne, Claude Monet, and renaissance painters like Agnolo Bronzino.
